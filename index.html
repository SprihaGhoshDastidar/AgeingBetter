About Us

Helping Hand connects young adult for companionship and transportation to seniors in need. It would provide assistance service to help people and their family members stay independent while living securely and happily at home. It would provide an affordable door-to-door transportation service and take you to the doctor's clinic, pharmacy or anywhere around town.

Its mission is to support families throughout the aging journey. Helping Hands also provides support and travelling facilities to visually disabled elders and seniors with walking sticks and wheelchair.

Our services

Run Errands

Get a ride to the bank, pharmacy or anywhere you need to go today.

Doctor/Pharmacy

Get a safe immediate ride to a doctor's or to the pharmacy to get your prescription.

Grocery Shopping

Go grocery shopping with our members while they carry your bags.
